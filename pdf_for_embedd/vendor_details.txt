### Espressif - ESP32-P4 / S3
*   **Target Application**: AIoT, Edge AI, HMI, Smart Home
*   **Detailed Specs**:
    *   **Processing**:
        *   **ESP32-P4**: High-performance Dual-core RISC-V (400MHz) with AI instruction extensions & FPU.
        *   **ESP32-S3**: Dual-core Xtensa LX7 (240MHz) with vector instructions for AI.
    *   **Memory**:
        *   **P4**: 768KB HP SRAM, 8KB TCM, support for external PSRAM/Flash.
        *   **S3**: 512KB internal SRAM, Octal SPI interface for Flash/PSRAM.
    *   **Video/Display**:
        *   **P4**: H.264 encoding support, MIPI-CSI (Camera) / MIPI-DSI (Display), LCD interface.
        *   **S3**: LCD interface (RGB/8080/6800), Camera interface.
    *   **Connectivity**:
        *   **P4**: Requires external module for Wi-Fi/BT (e.g., C6). Integrated USB 2.0 High Speed, Ethernet MAC (typically).
        *   **S3**: Integrated 2.4GHz Wi-Fi (802.11 b/g/n) and Bluetooth 5 (LE).
    *   **Electrical**:
        *   P4/S3: TSMC Low Power process (typically 40nm or 22nm depending on gen).
*   **Unique Features**:
    *   **P4**: Strong security, rich HMI (Touch, LCD), AI instructions.
    *   **S3**: Classic AIoT choice, integrated Wi-Fi/BT, widely supported in Arduino/ESP-IDF.
*   **Notes**: Competition allows P4 or S3. P4 is recommended for high-performance HMI/AI; S3 for connected low-power IoT.
